Who am I?

“He’s a hard-working, selfless student… I think he’ll do great in life”
(Julien Nal - Classmate, Oct 2024)




Over the years, I’ve learned and developed a love for technology. Whether that’s taking things apart or just learning my way around computers, tech has always influenced my life.

Born and raised in Dayton, OH, my upbringing was in a house of immigrant parents who always pushed me to discover new things and be my best. Since a young age, my mom would always describe me taking apart everything at the house and haphazardly putting it back together. As I grew older, I took on more major tasks such as job shadowing at WPAFB and becoming the first high school IT intern for Kettering Health.

My passions include photography and videography, general travel, programming, and working out.
